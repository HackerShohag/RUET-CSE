This research work is focused on detecting low-grade glioma tumorous cells in MRI images. Glioma is a common brain tumor, that exhibits properties of benign tumors. We used the TCGA-LGG Segmentation dataset for our research. It consists of 3929 brain tumor images and corresponding FLAIR abnormality segmentation masks obtained from 110 patients. \citation{wadhwa2019review}.

Table \ref{tab:encoder-models} lists the models used as encoder for U-Net architecture.
